BlockEstate is a secure, blockchain-powered real estate platform designed to eliminate fraud and middlemen from the flat-buying process.

Whether you're moving cities or investing remotely, you can browse verified listings from RERA-approved builders, compare market values, and even negotiate prices — all online.

Every ownership transaction is recorded on the blockchain, ensuring a tamper-proof record. Sale documents are stored permanently via IPFS, beyond the reach of manipulation.

Payments are safely handled through Razorpay-linked virtual accounts with full transaction traceability, and ownership transfers are completed within a day (T+1).

Can’t visit the flat in person? Use our agent-assisted on-site verification or video walkthrough service before making a decision — no guesswork, just clarity.
